## Naming Conventions

- **Files:** lowercase-with-dashes.md
- **Folders:** PascalCase or lowercase-with-dashes
- **Dates:** YYYY-MM-DD format
- **Versions:** filename-v2.md

## Daily Organization Tasks

1. **Morning:** Review TODO.md, update MORNING_IDEAS.md
2. **During Day:** Log activities to memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md
3. **Evening:** Clean up, move completed items, update TODO.md
4. **Weekly:** Archive old files, sync to Google Drive

## Email Organization (Gmail - viennathebot@gmail.com)

- **Inbox:** Action required
- **Starred:** Important/waiting
- **Labels:** Create for projects (DMV, Surrey Clinic, etc.)
