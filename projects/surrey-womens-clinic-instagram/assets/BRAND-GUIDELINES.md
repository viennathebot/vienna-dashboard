# Brand Guidelines — Surrey Women's Clinic

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Blush Pink:** #F5D6D0 — warmth, femininity
- **Lavender:** #E6E6FA — calm, sophistication
- **Sage Green:** #B2D3BE — health, wellness
- **Cream:** #FFF8F0 — clean, soft backgrounds

### Accent Colors
- **Deep Purple:** #6B4C7A — confidence, medical professionalism
- **Soft Gold:** #D4AF37 — premium feel (use sparingly)

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** #333333 (dark gray)
- **Secondary Text:** #666666 (medium gray)
- **Accent Text:** #6B4C7A (purple)

---

## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font:** Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond
- **Style:** Elegant, serif
- **Use:** Post headlines, quotes

### Body Text
- **Font:** Open Sans or Lato
- **Style:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Use:** Captions, descriptions

---

## Visual Style

### Photography
- Warm, natural lighting
- Soft focus, elegant composition
- Real women (diverse ages 35-65+)
- Avoid clinical/sterile imagery
- Spa-like, comfortable settings

### Graphics
- Clean, minimalist design
- Rounded corners, soft shapes
- Subtle gradients
- Consistent icon style
- White space for breathing room

### Avoid
- Harsh clinical imagery
- Overly bright or neon colors
- Stock photos that look fake
- Busy, cluttered designs
- Fear-based messaging

---

## Tone of Voice

### DO
- Be warm and compassionate
- Use empowering language
- Be professional yet approachable
- Speak directly to the reader
- Normalize intimate health conversations

### DON'T
- Use fear tactics
- Be preachy or condescending
- Use overly clinical jargon
- Make unrealistic promises
- Ignore the sensitive nature of topics

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Education** (40%) — Explain treatments, address myths, share facts
2. **Social Proof** (25%) — Testimonials, results, patient stories
3. **Engagement** (20%) — Relatable content, questions, community
4. **Promotion** (15%) — CTAs, booking, offers

---

## Brand Voice Examples

✅ **Good:** "You deserve to feel confident and comfortable in your own body."

❌ **Bad:** "Don't suffer in silence! Your problems can be fixed!"

✅ **Good:** "Many women experience this—and solutions exist."

❌ **Bad:** "Are you embarrassed by bladder leakage?"
