## Campaign Focus
Morpheus8 / InMode Treatments for women's intimate health

## Target Demographic
- Women aged 35-65+ in Surrey, Coquitlam, and Greater Vancouver
- Perimenopausal and menopausal women
- Post-childbirth women
- Women experiencing intimate health concerns

## Campaign Structure
- **20 posts** over ~40 days
- **Posting frequency:** Every 2 days (3-4 posts/week)
- **Platforms:** Instagram + Facebook
