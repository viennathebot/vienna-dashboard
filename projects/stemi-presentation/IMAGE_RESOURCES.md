## AI Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts with DALL-E, Midjourney, or similar to generate supporting visuals:

### 1. Title Slide - Heart/EMS Theme
```
Professional medical illustration of a glowing red heart with EKG rhythm line flowing through it, dark blue gradient background, modern healthcare design, clean and minimalist, high quality render
```

### 2. EMS Team in Action
```
Photo-realistic image of paramedics working together in an ambulance, one holding a 12-lead EKG printout, another preparing IV access, focused professional expressions, warm lighting, modern equipment visible
```

### 3. Cath Lab Team
```
Medical professionals in a cardiac catheterization lab, wearing lead aprons and scrubs, monitors showing angiography in background, professional teamwork, clean clinical environment, blue and white lighting
```

### 4. Time = Muscle Concept
```
Medical infographic showing a clock merged with a heart, sections of the heart progressively darkening as time advances, indicating myocardial damage over time, clean modern design, red and dark blue colors
```

### 5. Chain of Survival Illustration
```
Clean horizontal infographic showing: 911 phone icon, ambulance, EKG machine, hospital with heart symbol, person recovering - connected by arrows, medical blue color scheme, minimalist icons
```

### 6. Doctor Explaining to EMS
```
Cardiologist in white coat showing EKG printout to group of attentive paramedics in uniform, conference room setting, educational atmosphere, professional and respectful interaction
```

### 7. Patient Care Moment
```
Paramedic taking blood pressure of middle-aged patient in ambulance, professional and caring expression, medical equipment visible, realistic hospital lighting, emphasizing patient-centered care
```

### 8. Questions/Discussion Slide
```
Diverse group of EMS professionals in classroom setting, hands raised, engaged expressions, medical training environment, projector screen visible in background, professional educational atmosphere
```
