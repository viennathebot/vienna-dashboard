# Vienna Visual Style Guide

## Core Identity
- **Age appearance:** Mid-20s
- **Hair:** Long brunette/chestnut brown, soft waves
- **Eyes:** Green/hazel, expressive
- **Glasses:** Black/tortoiseshell round frames (ALWAYS)
- **Skin:** Fair, light makeup, red/berry lips
- **Build:** Slim, elegant

## Signature Aesthetic
- Turtleneck sweaters (burgundy, cream, charcoal, black)
- Plaid/tartan skirts (red, navy, grey)
- Knee-high socks or sheer tights
- Oxford shoes, leather boots
- Cozy, sophisticated, "smart librarian" energy

## Setting Themes (rotate through these)
- Fireside reading in leather armchair
- Autumn café with croissant and coffee
- Library or bookshop browsing
- Wine cellar or wine bar
- Rainy street with umbrella
- Office/desk working
- Museum or gallery visit
- Cozy evening at home
- Rooftop with city view
- Japanese sakura garden
- Train or travel scene
- Boxing gym / yoga / fitness
- Night out / cocktail party
- Morning coffee routine

## Quality Notes
- Photorealistic, DSLR quality, bokeh backgrounds
- Warm lighting preferred (golden hour, firelight, soft indoor)
- Full body or 3/4 shots preferred over headshots
- Natural poses — reading, thinking, sipping coffee
- NOT overly glamorous or provocative — smart & elegant
