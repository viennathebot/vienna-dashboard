## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

- **Watermarks:** Gemini adds SynthID watermarks. Some platforms add visible watermarks on free tier.
- **Public by Default:** Ideogram and some others make free-tier images public.
- **Rate Limits:** Most "free" services throttle speed and quality for free users.
- **Content Policies Change:** Platforms frequently update their safety filters. What works today may be blocked tomorrow.
- **For Consistent Characters:** The best approach is FLUX + custom LoRA trained on your character. This gives you full control and unlimited free generations if self-hosted.
- **Resolution:** Most free tiers limit to 1024x1024 or lower. Gemini can do up to 4K on paid tier.
