Residual SYNTAX Score: TAVR+PCI vs SAVR+CABG Gender-Based Comparison

Authors: Potratz M, Bleiziffer S, Rudolph TK, et al. (German Centers)
Journal: Open Heart | September 2025 | üîó PubMed (Free PMC)

Executive Summary

Propensity-matched comparison of residual SYNTAX scores and outcomes in TAVR+PCI vs SAVR+CABG, with gender-based subgroup analysis. Examines completeness of revascularization and its impact on outcomes.

Key Findings:

  • Residual SYNTAX score as a metric for revascularization completeness
  • Gender-specific outcomes analysis‚Äîimportant given different CAD patterns
  • TAVR+PCI increasingly used as traditional surgical approach
  • Addresses whether incomplete revascularization matters differently by approach
Residual SYNTAX after PCI matters. This study quantifies the impact‚Äîand suggests we shouldn't assume surgical revascularization always achieves complete revascularization either. Gender differences are worth noting.
