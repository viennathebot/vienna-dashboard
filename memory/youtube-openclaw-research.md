## Key Themes & Trends

1. **Naming evolution:** ClawdBot → MoltBot → OpenClaw (the 72-hour drama is a story in itself)
2. **Self-hosting is the killer feature:** Most tutorials focus on running it locally or on VPS
3. **Ollama integration** is the most requested/viewed setup type
4. **Comparison content** emerging (Nanobot, NanoClaw, Memubot vs OpenClaw)
5. **Enterprise/team use** starting to appear (Antigravity integration, AI CoWork)
6. **Platform integrations** popular: Telegram, Discord, iMessage, WhatsApp
7. **Free setup guides** get the most views (AWS free tier, Cloudflare, local)
